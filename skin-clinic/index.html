Aging is a Fact of life , looking like your age isn't

After acquiring his post graduate degree in Skin Diseases, Venereology & Leprology, Dr. Tannu started his career as a lecturer in B.J. Medical College & Sassoon General Hospital. With thrust to acquire further knowledge he then acquired diploma in the same science from Mumbai & Fellowship from Royal Society of Health, London. Later , Dr. Tannu Started private practices in Pune. He is also a life member of IMA (Indian Medical Association) and AFAASI (Association of Facial Aestheticians & Aesthetic Surgeons of India) with areas of interest in Microsurgery , Lasers and Procedural Dermatology. He has been awarded at many events and has also been a Visiting Specialist at many renowned hospitals including Inlaks & Budhrani, Ruby Hall Clinic & Police General Hospital.
